This is what an individual post looks like: a generous reading column, Century Schoolbook set for long-form text, and nothing on the page competing with the words. The drop cap and the date are the only ornaments.

Each post lives at its own address — this one is at `/writing/why-im-starting-this-blog/` — so readers can link to and share individual essays. The site also publishes an RSS feed at `/feed.xml`, so anyone using a feed reader is notified when you publish.

To write a new post, copy this file, rename it, change the title, description, and date at the top, and write in plain Markdown below the second `---`. That's the whole workflow.
